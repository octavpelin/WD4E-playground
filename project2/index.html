Mountain Expeditions

Here I am photo-documenting my expeditions in the mountains across Europe, with the Alps, the Scottish Highlands and the Carpathian mountains, and North America in the Appalachians.

In my search for the breathtaking and scenic landscapes, I found and photographed only the best. I usually use my Canon EF 16-35mm f/2.8L III USM lens for my Canon, so I can capture even the starry skies otherwise invisible to our daily life.
